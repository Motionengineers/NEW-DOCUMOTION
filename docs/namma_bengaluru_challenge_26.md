# Namma Bengaluru Challenge ‘26 — Brief

- Host: UnboxingBLR, in partnership with Social Alpha and WTFund; supported by Government of Karnataka (GoK) & Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA)
- Focus: Climate resilience for Bengaluru across Construction, Water & Sanitation, Air Pollution, Waste Management
- Who should apply: Innovators/entrepreneurs with deployment‑ready solutions (TRL6+), measurable impact, deployable in Bengaluru

## Program Benefits
- Pilot deployments: Up to ₹25 lakhs per start‑up for pilots (≈ 6 months) with implementation partners
- Market access & validation: Deploy within Bengaluru’s urban systems; pathway to wider adoption
- Showcase: Pitch to GoK agencies, industry and civil society
- Funding: Access to seed capital up to ~₹1 crore (subject to thesis alignment and diligence by Social Alpha)

## Important Dates (2025–26)
- Applications open: 6 Oct 2025
- Application deadline: 8 Nov 2025
- Evaluation: Nov 2025; Jury: Nov–Dec 2025
- Winners announced: Dec 2025
- Pilot preparation: Jan–Feb 2026
- Program implementation: Mar–Aug 2026

## Focus Areas (examples)
- Construction: Alternative low‑impact materials; C&D waste collection/sorting; performance enhancement of recycled materials
- Water & Sanitation: Drinking‑water quality/O&M; stormwater & sewage (decentralised STPs); O&M mechanisation; lake hyacinth/silt management
- Air Pollution: Low‑cost, energy‑efficient air purification with low maintenance
- Waste Management: Collection/upcycling; automated sorting; route optimisation; advanced/chemical/biological recycling to high‑value outputs
