# Startup Challenges, Events & Government Programs – India (2025–26)

This document provides a verified list of high-value Indian startup opportunities designed for founders building innovative products. These include government programs, national challenges, pitch competitions, and accelerator events.

---

## 1. Key Government Platforms

### 1.1 Startup India Portal

The official platform for:

- Registration
- Government challenges
- Tax benefits
- DPIIT recognition
- Grants, incubation & mentorship

Link: https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.2 National Startup Awards (NSA)

A flagship DPIIT initiative recognizing top Indian startups.

- Latest Edition: NSA 5.0
- Status: Application window opened and later closed (watch portal for next cycle).
- Categories: Agriculture, AI, Robotics, Fintech, Environment, Energy, Edtech, Health, and more.
- Benefits:
  - National recognition
  - Access to government buyers
  - Investor visibility
  - Media coverage

### 1.3 Bharat Startup Grand Challenge

A national platform where startups solve high-impact government and industry problems.

- Benefits include:
  - Expert mentorship
  - Pilot opportunities
  - Possible procurement pathways
  - National exposure

### 1.4 AI by HER – Global Impact Challenge (Women-led AI Startups)

A prestigious challenge focused on women innovators in AI.

- Prize: ₹2.5 million (₹25 lakh) per startup
- Winners: 10
- Deadline: 30 November 2025
- Theme: AI solutions for global impact
- Areas: Health, sustainability, education, climate, government services, accessibility, etc.

## 4. How Your Startup Should Use These Opportunities

### Step 1 — Register on Startup India

Required for most government-backed challenges.

### Step 2 — Track rolling applications

Important sources:

- Startup India challenges section
- F6S
- Devfolio
- Eventbrite
- Global Startup Summit platform

### Step 3 — Prepare these 3 core documents

- 3-minute pitch deck
- 1-page summary (USP, impact, traction)
- 2-minute product/demo video

### Step 4 — Apply to challenges that match your sector

Examples:

- AI Startup → AI by HER
- Sustainability Startup → Bharat Grand Challenge
- Mobility Startup → NSA categories

## Recognition, Challenges & Platforms — Status (Nov 2025)

Here are some of India's most prestigious startup challenges and platforms that offer mentorship, funding, and recognition. Statuses reflect current information as of November 2025.

### Open and Upcoming Opportunities

- **National Startup Awards (NSA 5.0)**
  - Host: Startup India (GoI)
  - Benefits: ₹10 Lakhs cash prize, handholding, mentorship, national visibility
  - Status: Open for applications (Oct/Nov 2025)

- **AI by HER: Global Impact Challenge**
  - Focus: Women-led AI startups (with WEP)
  - Benefits: ₹2.5 Mn total for 10 winners, visibility, mentorship, investor connects
  - Status: Open (Deadline: Nov 30, 2025)

- **TechSparks 2025**
  - Scope: Summit, showcases, masterclasses, hackathons, networking
  - Status: Upcoming (Nov 6, 2025)

- **Bharat Technology Conclave 2025 – Chennai**
  - Scope: Connects tech/SaaS startups with investors, branding, awards
  - Status: Upcoming (Nov 8, 2025)

- **StartupLive Bangalore ’26**
  - Scope: Team formation, mentorship, pitch showdowns, workshops
  - Status: Upcoming (Jan 30, 2026)

### Platforms & Ongoing Support Mechanisms

- **Startup India Hub**
  - Central portal for programs, challenges, mentors, incubators, funding

- **Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)**
  - Government initiative fostering innovation and entrepreneurship

- **Incubators and Accelerators**
  - Examples include IIM-linked incubators; support with incubation, seed funding, expert guidance

### Finding Opportunities

- Check the official **Startup India** portal regularly for live programs
- Track event platforms (e.g., F6S, Devfolio, Eventbrite, summit portals)
- Many opportunities offer more than funding—mentorship, market access, regulatory guidance
